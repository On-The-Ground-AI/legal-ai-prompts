# AI Ethics & Best Practices for Legal Professionals

## Overview
This section covers professional conduct, ethical obligations, and best practices for lawyers using AI tools in Singapore and APAC jurisdictions, aligned with Law Society guidelines and Singapore's MinLaw Draft Guide for Using Generative AI in the Legal Sector.
