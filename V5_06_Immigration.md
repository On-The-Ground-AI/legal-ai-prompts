# AI Prompts for Immigration Law

## Overview
This collection contains 30+ prompts covering work passes, permanent residence, citizenship, dependant sponsorship, and appeals under Singapore immigration law. These prompts support immigration professionals in advising employers and foreign workers on visa matters and Ministry of Manpower (MOM) compliance.

## WORK PASSES — EMPLOYMENT PASS (EP)
