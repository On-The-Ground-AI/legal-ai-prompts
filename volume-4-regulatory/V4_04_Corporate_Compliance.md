# AI Prompts for Corporate Compliance

## Overview
This library contains 35+ detailed prompts designed to assist legal, compliance, and business professionals with corporate governance and compliance matters in Singapore and APAC regions. Prompts cover anti-bribery and corruption, sanctions and embargoes, whistleblowing frameworks, ESG reporting and compliance, modern slavery and human rights, trade compliance and export controls, conflicts of interest management, and gifts and hospitality policies.
