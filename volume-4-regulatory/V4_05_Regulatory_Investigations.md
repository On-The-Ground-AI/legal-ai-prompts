# AI Prompts for Regulatory Investigations

## Overview
This library contains 22 detailed prompts designed to assist legal and compliance professionals in responding to and managing regulatory investigations in Singapore and APAC regions. Prompts cover responding to regulatory inquiries, internal investigation planning, privilege management, document preservation, witness protocols, investigation reporting, regulatory cooperation strategies, and remediation and enforcement prevention.

---

## Responding to Regulatory Inquiries & Requests

**PROMPT 1 — Analysis of Regulatory Information Requests**

We have received an information request from [INSERT REGULATOR: MAS, SGX, CCCS, PDPC, Singapore Customs, STB] in connection with [INSERT SUBJECT: investigation, examination, compliance review]. The request asks for [INSERT DOCUMENT/INFORMATION TYPES]. We must respond by [INSERT DEADLINE]. Please help us: (1) interpret the scope of the request—what documents and information are truly required vs. those we can reasonably exclude; (2) identify applicable legal bases for objecting—work product doctrine, privilege, business confidentiality, trade secrets, irrelevance; (3) assess completeness—are there documents/information we're missing that should be included; (4) evaluate producing vs. withholding—strategic risks of producing, legal risks of withholding; (5) prepare a detailed response plan identifying: (a) what we will produce, (b) what we will withhold with legal basis, (c) what clarification we need from regulator, (d) timeline for production; (6) quality assurance procedures—ensuring documents are accurate and complete; (7) organize production—document numbering system, production log, metadata preservation; (8) prepare cover letter—explaining response, discussing any objections/clarifications; (9) identify any gaps requiring additional information from business units; (10) escalation—determining what senior management/Board notification is required.

**PROMPT 2 — Privilege Protection & Legal Document Review**
