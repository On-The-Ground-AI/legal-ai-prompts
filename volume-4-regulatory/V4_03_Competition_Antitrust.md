# AI Prompts for Competition & Antitrust Law

## Overview
This library contains 25+ detailed prompts designed to assist legal, compliance, and business professionals with competition law matters in Singapore, ASEAN, and regional jurisdictions. Prompts cover merger notifications, cartel detection and analysis, abuse of dominance allegations, dawn raid preparation and response, leniency applications, market definition analysis, vertical agreements, and competition compliance programmes.
