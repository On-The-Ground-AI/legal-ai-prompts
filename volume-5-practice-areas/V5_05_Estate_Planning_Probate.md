# AI Prompts for Estate Planning & Probate

## Overview
This collection contains 30+ prompts covering will drafting, trust creation, powers of attorney, probate administration, and estate disputes under Singapore law. These prompts assist lawyers in advising clients on succession planning, managing estates, and resolving inheritance disputes.

## WILL DRAFTING & TESTAMENTARY PLANNING
