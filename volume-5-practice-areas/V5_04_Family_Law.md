# AI Prompts for Family Law

## Overview
This collection contains 35+ prompts covering divorce proceedings, custody and access disputes, maintenance obligations, matrimonial asset division, and family protection matters under Singapore law. These prompts support lawyers in advising clients, drafting pleadings and agreements, and navigating complex family law proceedings.

## DIVORCE PROCEEDINGS

**PROMPT 101 — Divorce Petition Drafting and Grounds Assessment**

I am advising a client who wishes to commence divorce proceedings against their spouse. The marriage is registered in Singapore and was solemnized on [DATE]. The client and spouse have been separated since [DATE - approximately X MONTHS AGO]. The client's ground for divorce is [SPECIFY: unreasonable behavior / adultery / desertion / 3+ years separation with consent / 4+ years separation without consent]. Please provide: (1) detailed assessment of viability of selected ground under Women's Charter Section 65; (2) if unreasonable behavior, analysis of specific incidents/behaviors that satisfy the "cannot reasonably be expected to live with" test established in case law; (3) if adultery, evidential requirements including corroboration (private investigator reports, communications, witness statements); (4) if desertion, requirements for proving 2+ years continuous abandonment with intent to bring cohabitation to end; (5) if separation-based, calculation of separation period and assessment of spouse's likely consent vs. opposition; (6) alternative grounds if selected ground appears weak; (7) documentation required to support petition including marriage certificate, separation evidence, behavior chronology; (8) strategic considerations in ground selection (e.g., unreasonable behavior allows faster resolution than 3-year separation even with consent); (9) draft petition incorporating selected ground with specific allegations and supporting facts; (10) procedural requirements including filing and service on respondent; (11) anticipated defense/response and how to address likely arguments; (12) financial/property implications arising from ground selection (e.g., impact on custody, maintenance, asset division). Provide complete draft petition ready for filing.

Context: At commencement of divorce proceedings
Difficulty: Advanced
Best AI tool: Claude
Follow-up: PROMPT 102 (Divorce defense), PROMPT 110 (Asset division)
