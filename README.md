## How to Use This Library

**Step 1:** Find the folder for your practice area or role (use the tables above).

**Step 2:** Open the file and browse the prompts. Each one has a title, difficulty level, and context note.

**Step 3:** Copy the prompt text. Replace anything in `[SQUARE BRACKETS]` with your specific details (jurisdiction, client type, document name, etc.).

**Step 4:** Paste into your AI tool (Claude, ChatGPT, Copilot, Gemini — any will work).

**Step 5: Review the output carefully.** Check every citation, every legal principle, every factual claim. AI makes mistakes. You are responsible for the final work product.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Do these prompts work with any AI tool?**
Yes. All prompts work with Microsoft Copilot, ChatGPT, Claude, Google Gemini, and most other AI assistants. No technical setup needed.

**Can I use AI output in court documents or client advice?**
You can use AI to generate a first draft. But every output must be reviewed, verified, and approved by a qualified lawyer. AI makes mistakes. You remain responsible for your work product. Many jurisdictions now require disclosure of AI use in court filings — check your local rules.

**Are these prompts specific to Singapore?**
The library is Singapore-first but globally usable. Most prompts include `[JURISDICTION]` placeholders so you can adapt them. Volume 7 (6 files, 170+ prompts) is entirely dedicated to Singapore and APAC law. Singapore-specific references appear throughout all volumes.

**Is it safe to paste client information into AI tools?**
Not in free/public AI tools. Use only your firm's approved enterprise AI tool, or anonymise all client details first. See the Data Security table above.

**Can I share these prompts with my team?**
Yes. This library is shared freely for use by legal professionals. If you adapt and redistribute, please credit The Legal Prompts Library.

**How often is this library updated?**
We update when significant regulatory changes, new AI tools, or new legal workflows make existing prompts outdated. Star this repo to get notified of updates.
