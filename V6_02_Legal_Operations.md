## A. MATTER MANAGEMENT

### PROMPT ID: LO-MM-001
**TITLE:** Matter Intake & Triage Protocol

**FULL TEXT:**
Create a matter intake and triage protocol for [REQUEST TYPE: litigation, compliance, transaction, etc.] that captures: (1) Request summary and business context, (2) Requester and approval authority, (3) Urgency and business impact assessment, (4) Resource requirements estimate, (5) Initial legal triage (matter type, complexity, precedent analysis), (6) Internal vs. outside counsel decision, (7) Estimated cost and timeline, (8) Decision on acceptance/prioritization, (9) Matter assignment and communication, (10) Formal matter file opening. **CONTEXT:** Effective intake processes ensure requests are properly assessed, appropriately resourced, and tracked. Poor intake creates lost matters, misaligned expectations, and inefficient resource allocation.

**DIFFICULTY:** Medium

**FOLLOW-UP:** Implement protocol; monitor triage decisions for accuracy; adjust criteria based on outcomes; use triage data to identify patterns or process improvements.
