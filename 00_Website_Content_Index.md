## Intro Paragraph (About This Library)

AI is changing how legal work gets done — but only if lawyers know how to use it well. A poorly written prompt gets a generic, unreliable answer. A well-crafted prompt gets a structured, professional output that saves hours.

This library gives you that head start. Every prompt in this collection has been designed for real legal tasks: drafting contracts, advising clients, managing compliance, preparing for hearings, and running a legal department. You don't need to be technical. You don't need to understand how AI works. You just need to copy, paste, and customise.

All prompts work with the tools you already have — Microsoft Copilot in Word, Outlook, and Teams, as well as ChatGPT, Claude, and other AI assistants.

## Important Disclaimer (Display Prominently on Every Page)

> ⚠ **These prompts are starting points, not legal advice.** AI tools can make mistakes, generate inaccurate legal statements, and miss jurisdiction-specific requirements. Every AI output must be reviewed by a qualified lawyer before use in any professional context. The lawyer or organisation using these prompts is solely responsible for any work product produced.
>
> **Data security:** Never paste confidential client information, client names, or privileged communications into a public AI tool (free ChatGPT, free Claude.ai, or any tool not approved by your firm or organisation). Always use your firm's approved enterprise AI tool, or anonymise all details first.

## FAQ Section

**Q: Do these prompts work with any AI tool?**
A: Yes. All prompts are written to work with Microsoft Copilot, ChatGPT, Claude, Google Gemini, and most other AI assistants. No technical setup is needed.

**Q: Can I use these prompts in court documents or client advice?**
A: You can use them to generate a first draft. But every AI output must be reviewed, verified, and approved by a qualified lawyer before use. AI can make mistakes. You remain responsible for your work product.

**Q: How big is this library?**
A: The full library contains 1,500+ prompts across 30+ files in 8 volumes. The quick-start packs give you role-specific prompts immediately. The full volumes go deep into every practice area with standardised prompt formats, difficulty ratings, and chaining suggestions.

**Q: Are these prompts specific to Singapore law?**
A: The main packs are jurisdiction-flexible with [JURISDICTION] placeholders. Volume 7 (4 files, 100+ prompts) is entirely dedicated to Singapore and APAC law. Many prompts across all volumes also include Singapore-specific references and context.

**Q: Is it safe to paste my client's information into AI tools?**
A: Not in public AI tools (free ChatGPT, free Claude.ai). Use your firm's approved enterprise AI tool, or anonymise all client details before using any public AI tool. Microsoft Copilot within your organisation's Microsoft 365 environment keeps data within your tenancy — confirm the settings with your IT team.

**Q: Can I share these prompts with my team?**
A: Yes. These prompts are shared freely for use by legal professionals. We ask only that you credit The Legal Prompts Library if you adapt and redistribute them.

**Q: How often is this library updated?**
A: We update the library when significant regulatory changes, new AI tools, or new legal workflows make the existing prompts outdated. Subscribe to our newsletter to receive updates.
